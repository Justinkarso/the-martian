The Martian

The Martian is a Sci-Fi set in the outer space, Where survival is the only thing on your mind. It doesn’t always work out.

About

The Martian is a community-driven survival MMO where players can play free, choosing their own path from countless options.

Experience planet exploration, immense PvE battles and a thriving planet world all availible at your fingertips.

Participate in many in-game professions and activities, including Exploration, Gathering, Crafting, Building and even terraforming!
